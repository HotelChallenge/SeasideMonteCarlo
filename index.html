About Us

Our idea was to create a place that provide an experience for all ages and for that people could feel comfortable, happy and satisfy with their experience. We saw that opportunity in creating a hotel that can make people feel amazed with what they see. So our idea was to create two different building but with the same name and one beside the other. For that to happen, we want to build the same concept but different environments, one being families, having all the services and amenities that they would need. The other building being for couples and adults, but people can have access to both sides.
